###### Tips:

1. Use `Download All` as you would a Save button to keep a local copy of your work backed up.
2. Hit `Ctrl` + `Shift` + `R` before starting a new extraction to clear existing data.
3. In case of an issue, log it [here](https://github.com/NIVANorge/STOPeData/issues) with screenshots and your extraction file.
4. Required input fields are bordered in <span style="color: red;">span</p>.
